Computer Science club is a club where any students with an interest in computer science can meet to develop skills to become a better programmer.

You don't need any programming experience to join, we will get you started and help you learn if you have any problems.

At club meetings, we participate in competitions, such as CTFs, and collaborate on projects. We meet in the Link Lab (Room 3016) every Tuesday after school from 3:40-4:55pm.

Join our Facebook group!

It is recommended that you have a Github account and a Cloud9 account to work on projects with us.
